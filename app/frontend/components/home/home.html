More about the issue

Technology has made great strides in the last 50 years. But, it’s surprising that it’s had little impact on diagnosing mental health disorders. Diagnosis is extremely inefficient. Practitioners spend weeks, months or even years analysing their patients. Sometimes, they’re even subconsciously biased towards one diagnosis over another, which hinders progress. What if we could automate the diagnostic procedure? What if the process was purely evidence-based? What if the machine could use the combined data from doctors over the world?
